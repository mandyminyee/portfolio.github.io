Employee Attendance System - FYP

I developed the Employee Attendance System, leveraging the PHP programming language to build an efficient and user-friendly platform. The purpose of the Employee Attendance System is to allow employers to track employee's attendance accurately such as clock in, clock out, overtime, and so on. By automating these processes, the system helps reduce errors and inefficiencies associated with manual attendance tracking. Additionally, it offers insightful reports and analytics to provide employers with a clear overview of attendance trends, ensuring better workforce management and compliance with company policies. The system is also designed to be user-friendly, offering a seamless experience for both employees and administrators.
