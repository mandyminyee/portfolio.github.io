Online Bakery Ordering Systems (Degree's Project)

My teammate and I collaboratively developed an Online Bakery Ordering System, leveraging the PHP programming language to build an efficient and user-friendly platform. I was responsible for creating a variety of essential pages, such as the Home page, About Us page, FAQ page, All Products page, Product Category page, Individual Product page, Search page, Admin Home page, Insert Product page, View Product page, and Delete Product page, ensuring a seamless experience for both users and administrators.
